# Bicycles-Sales-Analysis-Report

Bicycle Sales Data Analysis - Project

The document is a comprehensive report on the "Bicycle Company Sales Data Analytics Portfolio Project," prepared by Abraham Eninla using Microsoft Power BI.  It covers the analysis of sales data from 2024–2026, with a focus on transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights through data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboards.  The project includes five relational tables, seven derived analytical measures, and four dashboards analyzing performance by country, product category, individual products, and salespeople. 

Key highlights:

•	Business Performance: $3.51M gross revenue, $45.81K net profit, 7,000 units sold, and a 1.31% profit margin. 
•	Country Analysis: The US dominates with 65% of profits, followed by Canada (27.3%).  Other countries contribute only 7.5% of profits, indicating potential for growth in Europe and Australia. 
•	Product Category Analysis: Bikes account for 83% of profits, while Components contribute 12%, Clothing 4.6%, and Accessories less than 1%.  This highlights a heavy reliance on bikes and underperformance in other categories. 
•	Salesperson Analysis: The top 3 salespeople generate 40% of total revenue, indicating a talent concentration risk.  The bottom performers need mentorship and territory rebalancing. 

Critical Risks:

•	Low profit margin (1.31%) leaves the company vulnerable to cost increases. 
•	Heavy reliance on the US market (65% of profits) and Bikes category (83% of profits). 
•	Underperformance in Clothing and Accessories categories. 
•	Revenue concentration among a few top-performing salespeople. 

Strategic Recommendations:

1.	Improve profit margins to at least 3% within 12 months. 
2.	Diversify product offerings, focusing on Clothing and Accessories to achieve 10% revenue contribution within 18 months. 
3.	Optimize the sales team with incentives, mentorship, and territory rebalancing. 
4.	Expand geographically into Europe and the Pacific markets. 
5.	Promote a data-driven culture by expanding Power BI usage across departments. 

The report demonstrates advanced data analytics skills, including star schema design, DAX measure creation, interactive dashboard development, and business intelligence storytelling.  It is part of a professional LinkedIn portfolio and includes detailed documentation of the data model, KPIs, and strategic insights.
